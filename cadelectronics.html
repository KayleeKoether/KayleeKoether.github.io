Artist Statement

The project at hand was tasked to learn about 3D modeling and electronics, which has been achieved. The intention was to understand and demonstrate basic skills with cad and to come up with an idea and bring it to life. The model created would be 3D printed, then circuit boards would be made in order to give the physical model the ability to light up and become life-like. I chose to create an ice cream cone for this project, as I thought it would be fun and intricate to work with. The model would stand upright on a box provided, which would also hide away the circuit board and wiring, so it was especially important to think about that factor in the creation of it. I had to add other elements of an ice cream scoop and an ice cream tub for this specific reason, becuse the ice cream cone itself would not be able to support itself on its own. The other elements seem to fit nicely with the original, and even adds to the overall project. The project felt like a success in learning basic knowledge of modeling and electronics, as well as demonstrating that knowledge in a unique way.
