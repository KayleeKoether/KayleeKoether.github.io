I demonstrate my knowledge of Adobe Illustrator in this poster. It is typographical and it was important to pick a font and size to be repeated throughtout, especially with some sections, or steps, being longer than others.

I used Adobe Photoshop to create this collage. There are many different photographs pieced together, so I had to ajust each of them to look like they belonged togehter, such as with color adjustments. I also had to use composition principles when piecing them.

This is different from my other projects because it is not digital, although it shows my understanding of color combinations. I was able to use the color wheel to meet requirements and choose colors that would look good in relation to one another.
