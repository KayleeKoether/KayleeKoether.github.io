Artist Statement

I created this project, not only as a task, but to learn about the world of net art and engage with it as well. I had the opportunity to make multiple pages presenting my chosen concepts of net art. I was able to choose news articles of my preference, then rearrange a paragraph, creating sentences that did not make sense entirely, and create my art based off of that. My project was essentially multiple collages reflecting these sentences.

My knowledge has overall been expanded greatly and I have gained a new appreciation for this art form. Arists that create net art use effort and details to get their art the way they imagined, so it takes up a good amount of time. I now know this after creating my own net art.

The new information I learned and the new appreciations I have gained are essential to my career. I have essentially learned a new medium that could be useful in my career later on. The project also gives me a new aspect on art overall and that there are so many more possibilities than I could have imagined.
